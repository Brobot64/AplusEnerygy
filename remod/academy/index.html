About the Academy

APLUS Solar Academy is the training division of APLUS ENERGY SERVICES LIMITED, a fast-growing and reputable renewable energy company committed to advancing solar technology and sustainability in Nigeria.

We provide comprehensive theoretical, practical, and on-site installation training in solar energy solutions.

Our training programs are conducted in a conducive learning environment, led by experienced professionals, and enriched with hands-on practical sessions to ensure participants gain real-world expertise.

We emphasize interactive learning to foster a deep understanding of solar system design, installation, maintenance, and business opportunities in the renewable energy sector.

Upon completion, every trainee will be certified and accredited as an APLUS Solar Dealer, unlocking opportunities for partnership and business growth in the solar industry.

Join us at APLUS Solar Academy and take a step toward becoming a skilled professional in the renewable energy sector!
